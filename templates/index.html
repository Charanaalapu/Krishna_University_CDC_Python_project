College Development Council Department

Krishna University

Affiliated Colleges Fee Processing Unit





  • The major activities in the Academic and Audit Cell of the University are,
  • 1.Issue of Affiliation to affiliated colleges.
  • 2.Issue of Permanent Affiliation and Autonomy to affiliated colleges.
  • 3.Recommendations to UGC for grant of 2(f) & 12 (b) Status.
  • 4.NOC for Closure of Course(s) and Institution(s).
  • 5.NOC to Change of Location, Name of the College/Society.
  • 6.Hostel Maintenance in all colleges affiliated to Krishna University.
